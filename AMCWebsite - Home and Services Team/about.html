About

Welcome to Adelphi University's ACM (Association for Computing Machinery) website, where innovation and collaboration converge in the dynamic world of computing. As a hub for aspiring and seasoned computer science enthusiasts alike, our ACM chapter at Adelphi is dedicated to fostering a community that thrives on the cutting edge of technology. Whether you're a coding novice or a seasoned programmer, our website serves as a gateway to a plethora of resources, events, and opportunities designed to enrich your computing journey.

Join us in exploring the ever-evolving landscape of technology, where creativity meets functionality, and where Adelphi's ACM chapter paves the way for the next generation of tech leaders. Discover your potential, connect with like-minded individuals, and embark on a transformative experience that extends beyond the classroom. Welcome to Adelphi's ACM, where innovation meets community.
